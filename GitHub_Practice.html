African Lion Information

The African Lion is a lion species that is currently endangered in Africa

  • The average weight of the African Lion is between 265-420 lbs
  • The average height of a lion is 3.9 feet
  • African Lions are extinct in 26 countries and there are approximately 20,000 left in the world
  • Their color is usually orange-brown, yellowish-brown, or black

    • People have celebrated lions throughout history to embrace the courage and strength of them. People have taken different approches to help ensure that the African Lions do not go extinct
